About Us

Student Alumni Mentorship Program (SAMP) is the flagship initiative of Institute Alumni Relations Cell (IARC). Placement season is round the corner and it is natural for the students to panic and seek help from experienced persons. SAMP provides the current final year UG and PG students a platform to connect with last year passout students, who have “Been there, Done that”. With an experienced pool of over 199 mentors and a dedicated IARC team of 38 members that made this ambitious initiative a reality, we hope you’ll draw maximum benefits from this program and it will make the placements season more fruitful for you.
